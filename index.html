Publications

DEEP REINFORCEMENT LEARNING FOR ROBOT PATH PLANNING WITH PERIODICALLY MOVING OBSTACLES: CURRENT METHODS AND CHALLENGES

Published in Neuro Quantology, November 2022

We address the problem of path planning for mobile robots in dynamic environments. We propose a learning-based approach that leverages spatial and temporal data to overcome the limitations of traditional planning algorithms in the presence of moving obstacles. The paper provides a comprehensive survey of deep reinforcement learning (DRL) techniques and their application in various navigation scenarios, including obstacle avoidance and multi-robot navigation. We also discuss the evolution of DRL-based navigation and highlight the challenges and potential solutions in this field.
